S-Lines Welcome

Welcome to the Blog!

S-Lines Engine is about lines, all types of lines, Bezier curves, splines, polylines, etc. Create paths, follow those paths, make procedural animations or control the iteration state with a few keys.

This blog is going to cover different material related to demo projects as well as how to understand key concepts related to graphics and visualization. We will cover how algorithms can be used to create art within a graphics engine without choosing any specific one, by relying on abstractions.

This post is just an explanation of what you can see in the YouTube channel. Most of the videos include lines in one form or another.

The demos that have been made include some of the listed topics. Many of them, mix different concepts and use a wide range of algorithms to achieve its purpose.

  • Path Follow
  • Shapes Design
  • 3d modeling
  • procedural content
  • pattern recognition
  • animations
  • motion graphics

Keep reading the following blog posts for more about lines and the things you can do with them.
